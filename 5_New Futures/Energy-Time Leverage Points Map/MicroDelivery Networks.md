# Micro-Delivery Networks

## Description
Establish ultra-local fulfillment centers that enable same-day delivery of peak-freshness produce, reducing the time gap between selection and consumption. A more scalable version of a farmers market, can reach more people.: Expected Effect: Consumer get to have peak-season products that have more flavor and nutritional value, increasing the desire to eat more fresh produce     Example: 7/11 does this in Japan   #transition-areas
