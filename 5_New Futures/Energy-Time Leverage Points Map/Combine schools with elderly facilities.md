# Combine schools with elderly facilities.  - Opportunity for elderly care individuals to teach a healthy cooking session in schools for children to practise can lead to the expansion of cooking, learning of cooking skill and more communal experiences

## Description
Expected outcomes: Children gain confidence with new ingredients and techniques. Seniors experience pride and social inclusion by passing on traditions. Shared meals spark storytelling and social bonds.    #educational-and-food-pedagogy
