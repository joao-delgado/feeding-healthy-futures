[[Food Becomes Medicine for healthier lifestyles]]

# **What behaviors could be stimulated to foster a transition to a healthier eating habit?**


## Behavior:
Symptom Tracking and Body Awareness



### How:
When food is viewed medicinally, people pay closer attention to how different foods make them feel. This develops the habit of connecting dietary choices to energy levels, digestion, sleep quality, and overall well-being, creating a powerful feedback loop for positive change.Â
